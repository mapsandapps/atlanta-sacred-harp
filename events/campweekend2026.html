Singing The Weekend Before Camp Fasola 2026

Will you be in Atlanta on the Sunday before Camp Fasola 2026?

If so, you are invited to sing in downtown Atlanta on Sunday, 28 Jun 2026 from 1:00 to 4:00 PM.

Downtown Atlanta is about 20 minutes from the airport, 90 minutes from Camp Lee, and 110 minutes from Wellington, Alabama where the Monday "Fourth of July" singing will take place.

"Heedless Hall" is in downtown Atlanta 30312

DM David Brodeur or Andy Ditzler for directions

Sunday, 28 June 2026, 1:00 PM to about 4:00 PM

Two singing sessions from the Sacred Harp: 2025 Edition, with a longish break between.

Snacks and beverages are welcome, but don't feel obligated.

Campers and visitors are invited!
