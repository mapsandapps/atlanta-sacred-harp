Georgia Singings Map

This page has not been updated since 2019. See our Upcoming Singings page for current information or visit the map at shapenotesingings.com.

The map below shows the distribution of singing locations in Georgia and northeastern Alabama. To see a list of singings sorted by date, go to our annual singings page where you can click on the link for any singing for directions. Or see our upcoming singings page for details and verbal directions.

Marker color indicates the book used. The colors correspond roughly to book color: dark red for the 1991 Sacred Harp, dark blue for the Cooper Sacred Harp, white for the 1911 J. L. White Sacred Harp, dark gray for The Christian Harmony, light blue for The Georgian Harmony, and light green for The Social Harp. Gold markers indicate note singings. Some locations use more than one book.
