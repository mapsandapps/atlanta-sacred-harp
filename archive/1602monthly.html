February 2016 Singings

Updated February 18, 2016


Update: Corrected the start time of Feb 21 University of West Georgia singing to 10:00 am

How could one little month have so many great and varied singing opportunities? Lucky you! For example, the demonstration/lecture led by Jesse Karlsberg at Morningside Presbyterian on the 6th, the Emory singing on the 13th, SOFA singing Friday evening the 19th at Big Creek in Alpharetta, 3rd Sun. evening singing at Church of Our Savior features visiting singing school teacher Nathan Stewart, and of course, the "Eclectic Weekend" of Hardeman (J.L. White, Eclectic Harmony, Georgian Harmony) on the 27th, the Hollingsworths in Ila evening singing from Christian Harmony, and Athens singing the next day (28th) with Social Harp and Sacred Harp. And many more. Whew!

Feb. 4, 1st Thursday monthly singing, 7:30-9:30, in the Robinson Fellowship Hall, behind Emory Presbyterian Church, 1886 North Decatur Rd, Atlanta GA 30307. Directions: Half-mile west of the intersection of North Decatur Rd and Clairmont Rd, at the corner of North Decatur Rd and Westminster Way. Parking behind the church, off Westminster Way. Feb. 6, Sat. morning, come learn about Sacred Harp singing from 10 am to noon at the Morningside Presbyterian Church Fellowship Hall as Dr. Jesse P. Karlsberg of Emory University introduces the style's history and the music of its most popular collection of hymns, The Sacred Harp; then join in as we sing together from the tunebook, first published right here in Georgia in 1844. A potluck lunch will be provided at noon. Each participant is encouraged to bring a side dish or dessert. Meat and drinks will be provided. A donation will help the Atlanta Sacred Harp community continue their great work. All are welcome. Register by February 4 by contacting Jonathan Crutchfield - email jcrutchfield at morningsidepc dot org, or call extension 23 at 404-876-7396. Morningside Presbyterian is located at: 1411 N. Morningside Drive, NE, Atlanta, GA 30306. Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/events/756743601096786/
