July 2021 Singings

Updated June 28, 2021

July 1 - First Thursday Singing at Emory Presbyterian ‘Christmas in July’ singing. 7:00pm-9:00pm (or until it’s too dark!) We’ll plan to sing outside again (weather permitting), Emory Presbyterian Church, 1886 North Decatur Rd, Atlanta GA 30307. Park behind the church, off Westminster Way. We will be singing Christmas songs from The Sacred Harp, An American Christmas Harp, and The Eclectic Harmony. Feel free to bring copies of any 4-shape Christmas songs you like. Holiday snacks and attire encouraged! Info: David Brodeur, david -at- davidbrodeur -dot- com or Lisa Bennett, (404) 402-9562, AtlantaSinging -at- gmail -dot- com

July 3 & 4, 1st Sunday and Saturday before, 9:00 am Central Time on Saturday, 9:30 am CT on Sunday. Henagar-Union Convention, Liberty Church, 2 miles north of Henagar AL on Liberty Road. From Henagar Crossing, take Hwy 40 west to the Farmers Telephone Cooperative. Turn right and go to dead end. Turn right, take first left past Henagar Methodist Church (Liberty Rd). Church is on right, approximately 1.5 miles. Info: David Ivey (256) 258-8660, david -at- davidivey -dot- com

July 10, Saturday before 2nd Sunday, 10:00 am, Flat Shoals Primitive Baptist Church, 2052 Fairview Road, Stockbridge, GA. Info: Jesse Roberts, (678) 386-6438 jroberjr -at- bellsouth -dot- net. 10am-3pm with dinner on the grounds.

Note: Please note that if you have not been vaccinated, and you want to come, make sure that you are not running a high fever, you’re not coughing, or you’re not experiencing shortness of breath (SO2 level should be 90 or higher); if so, stay home. If you are symptom-free and have not been vaccinated, we request that you wear a mask at all times (except while eating) and practice social distancing. The square will be set up with the chairs 8” apart, and we expect you to spread out the same way if you’re sitting on benches. The leader will be 10’ away from the first row of singers (standing on an X on the floor). At lunch, sanitize your hands before you set out your food, and sanitize them at the front of the lunch line before you get your plates, eating utensils, and napkins. Drinks will be poured and handed to you. If you go back for seconds, throw your first plate and utensils away and start the process over. If anyone has a tailgate tent, please bring it; we plan to set up some tailgate tents outside the dining area, so we can distance some while eating.
