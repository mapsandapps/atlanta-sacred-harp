October 2019 Singings

Updated October 18, 2019

Sacred Harp Singings are free and open to the public. All are welcome (including children of all ages). The only requirement for singing Sacred Harp is the desire to sing Sacred Harp. This is a participatory style of singing, not a concert or performance, so you are always welcome (and encouraged!) to join in. An offering is usually taken to defray expenses.

For “All-Day” singings:

There will be a Dinner on the Grounds around noon (potluck) unless otherwise noted. Please stay and eat even if you didn’t bring food. There is always enough to go around.

Singing start times are noted, but end times vary depending on how many singers/leaders attend. You can usually expect “All-Day” singings to end between 2:00 and 3:00 pm.

All singings are from The Sacred Harp 1991 edition unless otherwise noted. For Google maps for all these singings, please see the links on the pages for annual singings, local groups or note singings. For easier reading of the listings, driving directions for most singings have been moved to the bottom of this page.

Driving Directions

Monthly Atlanta Area Singings:

Emory Presbyterian (1st Thursday)

Half-mile west of the intersection of North Decatur Rd and Clairmont Rd, at the corner of North Decatur Rd and Westminster Way. Parking behind the church, off Westminster Way.

Holy Trinity Episcopal (2nd Tuesday)

515 East Ponce de Leon Ave, Decatur GA 30030, three blocks east of the Decatur MARTA station. Parking lot entrance is on Sycamore Street. Enter the parish hall through the red doors facing Sycamore Lane.

Church of Our Saviour (3rd Sunday)

Garrison Hall (building across from main church entrance) 1068 North Highland Ave. Atlanta, GA 30306. Parking along N. Highland or Los Angeles. Metered spaces are free on Sunday.

Annual (All-Day) Singings:

Poplar Springs Primitive Baptist Church (Union Convention):

6465 Smithfield Rd, Bowdon GA. From I-20, take exit 5 at Highway 100, and drive 7.2 miles south. Turn right onto Smithfield Rd. The church is on the right.

Put another way: The church is approximately 6.3 miles south of Interstate 20 Exit 5. At Exit 5 turn south towards Bowdon on GA Highway 100 and drive about 4.7 miles. You will pass Kansas Baptist Church on your left. Just beyond this, about level with the far edge of the cemetery, turn right on Lovvorn Mill Road. Drive about 1.6 miles to the 4-Way Stop at the crossroads with Smithfield Road. At this turn right on Smithfield Road. The white church is a short way along on the right. If you miss the Lovvorn Mill Road turning continue south on Highway 100 for a further 2.4 Miles to the 4-Way stop at the crossroads with Smithfield Road. At this Stop sign turn right on Smithfield Road. Drive about 1.4 Miles to the church on the right, a little beyond the 4-Way Stop crossroads with Lovvorn Mill Road.

Liberty Hill Community Center

From Atlanta, go south on Interstate 75 to exit 201. Turn right (west) onto Georgia Highway 36 and go about 7 miles. Liberty Hill Church will be on the left in a small grove of oak trees; the Community Center will be on the right.

Muscadine Methodist

Take exit 5 from I-20, north to Tallapoosa, turn right on Hwy. 78, go about 7 miles past the border with Alabama to Muscadine, turn south on County Rd. 49, go 1 mile, church is on the right.

Andrews Chapel Schoolhouse

Directions from I-75: Take either the High Falls exit #198 or the Barnesville/Jackson exit #201, and head west. Once on Ga. Hwy. 36, continue south through Barnesville. About 8.5 miles south of Barnesville, turn left (south) on The Rock Rd. Cross Hwy. 74 and drive to the stop sign. Turn left, east, on Andrews Chapel Rd. The church will be about a mile away, on the right.

Directions from Thomaston: From Georgia Highway 74 leaving Thomaston heading east, turn right, south, on The Rock Rd and drive to the stop sign. Turn left, east, on Andrews Chapel Rd. The church will be about a mile away, on the right.
