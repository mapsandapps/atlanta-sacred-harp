2021 Singings

Updated February 2, 2021

It's safe to assume that singings are cancelled unless announced otherwise. We will post any information re: specific singings (either being cancelled or being held) as long as you send it to us (Georgia singings only or if out of state, a convenient drive from the Atlanta area). Stay safe (and warm!!)

January

January 1 - The New Year's Day Georgian Harmony singing held at the home of John and Elsie Hollingsworth is cancelled for this year. Plan to join us Jan 1, 2022!

January 30, Saturday before the 5th Sunday - The annual Georgian Harmony singing scheduled for the Saturday before the 1st Fifth Sunday will go ahead as scheduled on Saturday, January 30th, at the Civic Center in Roberta, GA. (S. Matthews Street) 10am start.. Info: Oscar McGuire. Will have social distancing as possible, fans to circulate air, etc. Lunch will be provided (vegetable soup, corn bread, and a few subway sandwiches for lunch). PLEASE TEXT Oscar at 478-955-6135 with any questions, concerns, etc.
