June 15, 5:30–6:00pm., Thursday. Join us for a special half hour of singing with the members of the American Theological Library Association at Cannon Chapel on the campus of Emory University. Organizers expect upwards of 200 conference attendees for the singing, and they are evidently a musical bunch. We'll have just the briefest of introductions and then take turns leading songs from a packet of selections from the 1991 Edition, hopefully with a few conference attendees joining each of us in the hollow square. After the singing, Sacred Harp singers are invited to join the librarians for supper, catered by Bell Street Burritos, at the Pitts Theology Library. Please arrive by 5:00 pm. Parking is available in the Oxford, Peavine, and Fishburne decks. Contact Jesse P. Karlsberg (jesse at jpkarlsberg dot com, 617-984-9378) for information.
