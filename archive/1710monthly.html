Mark your calendars: The annual Atlanta Sacred Harp Christmas gathering will be the December 17th, 3rd Sunday in December at Church of Our Saviour in Virginia-Highland (same as last year). 3pm-7pm-ish. As in the past we’ll use our regular books (SH & EH1) + the American Christmas Harp and any other 4 shape tune (seasonal) you may want to bring. Sing from 3p-5p, break for a meal and then sing another hour or so after that.
