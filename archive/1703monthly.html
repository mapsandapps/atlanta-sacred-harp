Update: Got the days right but the dates wrong for the Georgia State Sacred Harp Convention. It's now fixed here. The Facebook Page is now up and has it right: https://www.facebook.com/events/949348108535241/

March on out to some of these fine singings. Check out the "Songs Abound" singing March 24, the Georgian Harmony singing in the afternoon on March 19 at Hardeman, or take a trip to sunny Sarasota Florida for a new singing March 11, or any singing you choose, large or small, near or far!
