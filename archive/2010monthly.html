2020 Singings

Updated October 24, 2020

I hope everyone is well and staying safe. Here are the changes/updates for some of the upcoming singings near the metro Atlanta area and other parts of Georgia. Upcoming Singing Updates (Unless otherwise noted, singings are expecting to resume in 2021)

September

September 27th, Fourth Sunday - Poplar Springs Primitive Baptist Church, Carroll County GA: 10am start. info: Cecil Roberts (contact info in minutes). Unaware of any particular plans for the singing other than they plan to hold it. In addition to Cecil this number is listed on the Poplar Springs Facebook page: (770) 845-0716 https://www.facebook.com/PoplarSpringsPBCBowdon

October/November

October 3rd, Saturday before First Sunday - Old Shady Grove Baptist Church, between Dutton and Section AL. Just off the north side of Hwy. 71 (Cooper Revision). Info: Syble Adams. Cancelled in 2020

October 4th, First Sunday - South Georgia Convention - Convention will be held at the Roberta Community Center in Roberta, GA. for 2020, 10am start.. Info: Oscar McGuire. Will have social distancing as possible, fans to circulate air, etc. Oscar will order lunch - PLEASE TEXT HIM AT 478-955-6135 if you would like him to order you a lunch (otherwise plan to bring a sack lunch or see what local Roberta restaurants might be open)

October 18th, Third Sunday - Andrews Chapel, 6.1 miles south of Thomaston GA on Hwy 74. Turn right on The Rock Road to first stop sign, bear left, 1 mile to school. BRING A SACK LUNCH!! This will be a memorial singing for Bentley McGuire. Contact Oscar re: questions, etc., 478-955-6135 (text is probably best)

October 24th, Saturday before Fourth Sunday - Seed and Feed Sacred Harp Singing - Northlake Church of Christ, 1625 Cooledge Rd, Tucker GA. CANCELLED FOR 2020.

October 25th, Fourth Sunday - Coweta County Courthouse, Newnan GA. CANCELLED

October 31st, Saturday before First Sunday - Denney Memorial - Emmaus Primitive Baptist Church, Carrollton, GA. Info: Philip Denney 770-367-2628 Please contact Philip for details re: precautions. Here are some of his plans for the singing: The singing at Emmaus just happens to have available all the capabilities to make for a very, very safe singing. I think we would require that a maximum of 60 persons be allowed in the building at any given time. In the unlikely event the attendance is greater, then outdoors singing would be the obvious choice. If there is rainy weather AND a large turnout I propose a shortened day with volunteers singing under the pavilion. Fans to circulate air, hand sanitizer, wear/bring a mask, and he will have a pair of clear plastic shields standing in the leaders square for the safety of leaders. I think Philip plans to have lunch catered and served, so please contact him if you plan to attend so he can have a head count.

November 1st, First Sunday - Holly Springs Primitive Baptist Church, Bremen, GA. Info: Philip Langley. CANCELLED FOR 2020

December

December 5th and 6th, First Sunday and Saturday before - The Georgia Christian Harmony Convention is cancelled for 2020.
