October 2016 Singings

Updated October 29, 2016


Update: Noted that the Union Convention starts at 10:00 on Sunday, October 30.

At last, we are into autumn - what a glorious time to go to singings! Note the special demo singing on the 7th near Sparta, Ga. (especially if you can help), the Seed and Feed on the 22nd (my favorite, and a very welcoming local singing for new and experienced singers), and the Union Convention on the 29th-30th.
