September 2025 Singings

Updated August 26, 2025

Aug 27, Wednesday, 7:00pm - Dahlonega Sacred Harp singing, We sing at the Vickery House on West Main Street, Dahlonega, GA. The Vickery House does not have a street address, but it is on Google Maps. Since we sing in the evenings, you can park in any UNG spot or right next to the back door of the Vickery House. We start with a brief introduction for newcomers at 6:45. Then we sing until at least 8:45, although sometimes we go a bit longer. All are welcome! Contact Esther Morgan-Ellis - emellis at gmail dot com.

Aug 30, Sat (one time date change) - Greenville-(Pioneer Day) Sacred Harp Singing: The Church of Greenville, 228 Ladean Ct, Simpsonville, SC 29680, 10:00 am - 3:00 pm. Dinner on the grounds. We have loaner books and books for sale. Practice whatever cautions you feel are appropriate. Out of consideration for others, if you have cold/allergy symptoms please do not attend. Directions from I-385 in Simpsonville, SC: Take exit 27 and turn west. Go past Granview Dr and turn left on Ray E Talley Ct. Turn right on Ladean Ct. Church is at the end of the road past the cul-de-sac. CONTACT: Robert Kelley: composer at mykeyboard dot com

Aug 31, 5th Sunday, Lacy Memorial, Fuller Cemetery, Ider AL 9:30 am Central Time, Info: Reba Dell Windom, brwindom -at- hotmail -dot- com, 256-632-6386.

Sept 1, Monday, 9:30 am Central Time, Labor Day Singing/Cleburne County Convention - Shoal Creek Church, Talladega National Forest, near Edwardsville, AL. Now combining with the Cleburne County Convention. Dinner on the grounds at noon.. GPS: 33.775774, -85.564088 (note: cell coverage is weak/spotty)

Sept 4, First Thursday, 7:00 PM to 9:00 PM. Emory Presbyterian Church, 1886 North Decatur Rd, Atlanta GA 30307. Park behind the church, off Westminster Way. Sacred Harp and Eclectic Harmony. Info: Jim Neal at jim_neal-at-bellsouth-dot-net.

Sept 6, Sat before 1st Sunday, 10:00am - Georgian Harmony Singing, Roberta Civic Center, 93 South Mathews Street, Roberta GA. Directions: From Atlanta, I-75 South to I-475 South. Turn right/west onto Hwy 80W (Eisenhower Parkway) to Roberta. Or, from Macon, take Hwy 80W to Roberta. In Roberta, turn left/south on N. Mathews Street. It becomes S. Mathews Street when you cross over Agency Street. The Civic Center (an old wooden church with good acoustics), is located at 93 South Mathews Street, just south of the old part of the town, next to the recycling center. S Mathews St is one block east of Highway 341. Contact/info: Oscar McGuire. 478-955-6135 (text preferred) - Contact Oscar with any questions.

Sept 6, Saturday, 9:30a-2:00p - Please join us for a special Sacred Harp singing at the fourth annual Kennedy Creek Old-Time Music Festival! This singing will take place in the events pavilion at the Kennedy Creek Resort in Suches, GA. Bring a picnic lunch or plan to eat in the restaurant. The singing will wrap up at 2 pm so that the fiddle contest can start. There is a square dance in the evening at 7 pm. There is no admission fee for the festival. Address: 3001 High Valley Road, Suches, GA 30572 If you want to stay the night before or after the singing, please book a campsite with the Kennedy Creek Resort. To see the full festival schedule, visit https://morgan-ellis.net/kennedy-creek/ Contact Info: Esther - emellis at gmail dot com

Sept 12, 13 14, United Sacred Harp Musical Association, 2nd Sunday and Saturday before. The debut of the 2025 Revision of the Sacred Harp:

  • Sept 12th - A symposium on the process of revising the Sacred Harp. Held at Holy Trinity Parish in Decatur, GA. Registration required, $25.
  • Sept 13, 14 - United Sacred Harp Musical Association, The Foundry @ Puritan Mills. Doors open at 8:00 - Come get your 2025 edition!! - 9:30am start singing. We are anticipating hundreds of singers for this historic event. Please register so we have a good headcount. Lunch will be catered. Local singers, please bring a dessert. (maybe more than one!!)

Sept 20, Saturday before 3rd Sunday, 10:00 am, Lee Rogers Memorial Christian Harmony Singing. Singing at No. 3 Railroad Street, Arnoldsville, GA. Dinner on the grounds at noon. 10:00 a.m.-3:00 p.m. Mike Spencer: fishman.mds at gmail dot com

Sept 21, 3rd Sunday, 10:30 am, J.L. White book, B.F. White Memorial Sacred Harp Singing at Hardeman Primitive Baptist Church, 3965 Glenwood Rd., Decatur, GA, just west of the intersection of Glenwood Rd and Columbia Drive in Decatur. Singing from the J.L. White 1911 edition of the Sacred Harp (loaner copies will be available). Dinner on the grounds at noon.

Sept 27th, Mountain Heritage Day, Western Carolina University, Cullowhee, NC. Starts at 11am and ends at 3pm. We'll do Sacred Harp at 11 and Christian Harmony at 1:30, each for an hour and a half. We'll be located in front of the Bardo Fine and Performing Arts Center, which is across the creek from our traditional location. We're near the car show, demonstration areas, and food tents. The crafts are across the creek where the car show used to be. Parking will be toward the South end of campus. There will be directional signs all over the place when people get here! Please contact Will Peebles for details. Wpeebles at email dot wcu dot edu
