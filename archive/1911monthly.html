November 2019 Singings

Updated October 31, 2019

Sacred Harp Singings are free and open to the public. All are welcome (including children of all ages). The only requirement for singing Sacred Harp is the desire to sing Sacred Harp. This is a participatory style of singing, not a concert or performance, so you are always welcome (and encouraged!) to join in. An offering is usually taken to defray expenses.

For “All-Day” singings:

There will be a Dinner on the Grounds around noon (potluck) unless otherwise noted. Please stay and eat even if you didn’t bring food. There is always enough to go around.

Singing start times are noted, but end times vary depending on how many singers/leaders attend. You can usually expect “All-Day” singings to end between 2:00 and 3:00 pm.

All singings are from The Sacred Harp 1991 edition unless otherwise noted. For Google maps for all these singings, please see the links on the pages for annual singings, local groups or note singings. For easier reading of the listings, driving directions for most singings have been moved to the bottom of this page.

Annual (All-Day) Singings:

Emmaus PBC (Denney Memorial)

Take I-20 West to GA exit #11. Go South on U.S. Hwy. 27 through Carrollton for about 13.5 miles. Turn right on Oak Grove Rd., go about 1/2 mile. Turn Left onto Oak Grove Church Rd. and go about 1/2 mile again. The meeting house will be on the left.

Holly Springs

Directions: Take exit #11 from I-20, turn south on Hwy. 27, go to top of hill at service station, turn left on Holly Springs Rd., go to dead end and turn left, church is on left.

Oak Hill Baptist (Calhoun County and Boiling Springs Convention)

Get on Highway 21 South in Oxford AL, go under I-20. Take a left at light onto Friendship Road. Cross 3 bridges. At next traffic light turn right onto Chelsea Drive. Go 5-6 blocks. Turn left onto County Line Rad. Church is on right.

John & Elsie Hollingsworth’s house (Georgian Harmony):

1574 Adams Clarke Rd, Ila GA. Directions: From Athens: Take Hwy 29 north, towards Danielsville. At the light near the Ingles store, bear left onto Hwy 106. Just past the 9-mile marker, turn left onto Adams Clarke Rd (at the Hill's Automotive sign). Go 1.5 miles to mailbox on right numbered 1574. When driveway splits, bear left. From I-85 North: Take Exit 149 to Hwy 441 at Banks Crossing outlet malls. Turn right onto 441 South. Go about 5 miles to Hwy 98 and turn left. In about 8 miles you'll reach a 4-way stop in Ila. Turn right and go 0.8 mile, then turn right on Adams Clarke Rd, go 1.5 miles to mailbox on right numbered 1574. When driveway splits, bear left.

Jefferson State Community College (Alabama State SH Convention)

From Downtown Birmingham or South. Take I-59 to the Tallapoosa Street / AL 79 exit. Turn north on AL 79 and drive 8.7 miles to Carson Road. Turn right on Carson Road. Drive around past GCW Hall on your right, and continue around back to the Eugene Fitzgerald Student Center. (The east entrance to campus is usually locked up for the weekend, so stay to the west side.)
