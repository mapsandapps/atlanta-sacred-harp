Camp FASOLA is back! Camp Fasola announces a change of venue for 2023 to Camp Lee in Anniston. This change enables us to hold both Adult Camp and Youth Camp at the same location in the same week. Adults have the option of lodging offsite in hotel accommodations. Camp dates remain the same: Monday, July 3 to Friday, July 7. Registration is extended. Details and online registration is available at CampFasola.org. You can also register by mail by downloading the registration form from the website. Please let us know if you have questions: camp@fasola.org Y'all come! David Ivey
