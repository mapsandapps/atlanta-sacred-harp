Sept 5, First Thursday, 7:00 PM to 9:00 PM. Indoor singing with COVID antigen testing. Emory Presbyterian Church, 1886 North Decatur Rd, Atlanta GA 30307. Park behind the church, off Westminster Way. "test-to-sing" COVID precautions: Don't come if you have any COVID symptoms; same-day COVID antigen test required. Test at home and bring the details to be verified. Tests available on site for a fee, if you arrive early. Info: Jim Neal at jim_neal-at-bellsouth-dot-net.

Sept 7, Saturday, 9:30a-2:00p - Please join us for a special Sacred Harp singing at the fourth annual Kennedy Creek Old-Time Music Festival! This singing will take place in the events pavilion at the Kennedy Creek Resort in Suches, GA. At 9:15 Sasha Hsuczyk and Esther Morgan-Ellis will offer a brief introduction for newcomers, and at 9:30 we'll start the singing. Bring a picnic lunch or plan to eat in the restaurant. The singing will wrap up at 2 pm so that the fiddle contest can start. There is a square dance in the evening at 7 pm. There is no admission fee for the festival. Address: 3001 High Valley Road, Suches, GA 30572 If you want to stay the night before or after the singing, please book a campsite with the Kennedy Creek Resort (https://www.kennedycreekresort.net/). To see the full festival schedule, visit https://morgan-ellis.net/kennedy-creek/ Contact Info: Esther - emellis at gmail dot com or Sasha - protectorofthesmall at gmail dot com.
