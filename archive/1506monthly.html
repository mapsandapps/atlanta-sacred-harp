United & Associated Note Singings:

For more information about this 7-shape African-American singing tradition in Georgia, check out this article: http://www.westga.edu/assetsDept/pubhistory/Background.pdf. Something different from the Sacred Harp and Christian Harmony traditions, but with its own power and community. Unless otherwise noted, these are typically held 2:00-4:00pm.
