August 2022 Singings

Updated July 28, 2022

As there is an upswing in coronavirus cases recently, if you are thinking about attending a singing and have any questions, please contact the person listed to confirm.

Jul 31, Lacy Memorial Singing, Fuller Cemetery, Ider AL 9:30 am Central Time, Info: Reba Dell Windom, brwindom -at- hotmail -dot- com, 256-632-6386.

Aug 4, First Thursday, 7:00 PM to 9:00 PM. Indoor singing with COVID antigen testing. Emory Presbyterian Church, 1886 North Decatur Rd, Atlanta GA 30307. Park behind the church, off Westminster Way. NEW "test-to-sing" COVID precautions: Don't come if you have any COVID symptoms; same-day COVID antigen test required. Test at home and bring the details to be verified. Tests available on site for a fee, if you arrive early. Details: https://bit.ly/3I9ePuK Info: David Brodeur, david .at. davidbrodeur -dot- com or Amy Wells, amyarmstrongmidwife-at- gmail -dot- com.

Aug 6-7, 1st Sunday and Saturday before, 9:30 am, 170th Chattahoochee Sacred Harp Convention, Wilson's Chapel, 289 Hutcheson Ferry Rd, Whitesburg, GA 30185, southeast of Carrollton, GA. GPS: 33.557058, -84.966422 Info: Lisa Webb - 770-832-3918 or Laura Webb - 770-823-1404. Driving directions:

  • Option 1: Take I-20 to Hwy 61, south to Carrollton. Turn left at the 166 bypass (there are 2 places to turn onto 166, take the 2nd turn... it's a big intersection), go about 1 mile and turn left (south) on Old Newnan Road. Old Newnan will eventually come to a fork and bear to the right, you want to go straight (left) onto Cross Plains Road. Go past Cross Plains Baptist Church and the old School House; at the 3 way intersection jog slightly to the right and take the dirt road, which is Hutcheson Ferry Road, Wilson's Chapel is about 100 yards down the dirt road on the left.
  • Option 2: I-20W to exit #30/Post Road (just past Douglasville), go south until you get to Highway 166, turn right onto 166... you'll stay on this for a good ways, don't worry about any turns until you get into Carroll County. Look out for Cross Plains-Hulett Road (there is a convenience store on the right), and turn left onto Cross Plains-Hulett Road. It will eventually intersect with Cross Plains Road and when it does, turn left onto Hutcheson Ferry Road (the dirt road). Wilson's Chapel will be on the left. John's been going this way a few years, thinks it might be a little shorter.
