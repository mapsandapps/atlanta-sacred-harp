January 2016 Singings

Updated January 26, 2016


Update: We now have the location for the Biweekly singing on January 27. This will be the regular location from now on. Check it out if you haven't already!

January has some interesting opportunities for singing if the weather permits you to travel a bit (or, just stay home and catch up with friends at our local regular evening singings). I included the Faircloth Memorial Cooper Book singing in Bonifay, FL on the 17th - a bit far, but it's on the panhandle and might be a nice time to go down there. Note the Camp Fasola dates at the end - it won't be too early to sign up.

Dec. 31, Thurs., New Years Eve Singing at the home of John and Elsie Hollingsworth, Ila Ga. Start at 6:00 p.m. and "sing the year out". Singing from The Sacred Harp, 1991 ed. and Christian Harmony (7 shape). Directions: From Athens: Take Hwy 29 north, towards Danielsville. At the light near the Ingles store, bear left onto Hwy 106. At the next light, about 1 mile, turn right to stay on 106 toward Ila. Just past the 9-mile marker, turn left onto Adams- Clark Road (at the Hill's Automotive sign). Go 1.5 miles to mailbox on right numbered 1574. When driveway splits, bear left. From I-85 North: Take Exit 149 to Hwy 441 at Banks Crossing outlet malls. Turn right onto 441 south. Go about 5 miles to Hwy 98 and turn left. In about 8 miles you'll reach a 4-way stop in Ila. Turn right and go 0.8 mile, then turn right on Adams-Clark Road, go 1.5 miles to mailbox on right, no. 1574; when driveway splits, bear left. Contact: John Hollingsworth, john -at- billnet dot org, (706) 789-3916.
