2020-2021 Singings

Updated December 14, 2020

I hope everyone is well and staying safe. Here are the changes/updates for some of the upcoming singings near the metro Atlanta area and other parts of Georgia. Unless otherwise noted, singings are expecting to resume in 2021.

November

November 1st, First Sunday - Holly Springs Primitive Baptist Church, Bremen, GA. Info: Philip Langley. CANCELLED FOR 2020

November 14th, Saturday before the 3rd Sunday - The last Georgian Harmony singing for 2020 will relocate from the Hollingsworth’s house in Ila, GA to the Civic Center in Roberta, GA, as there is more space to have some distancing in the set-up. Contact Oscar McGuire re: details and any questions you might have. 478-955-6135 (text is probably best)

November 28/29, 5th Sunday and Saturday before - the 152nd session of the Union Sacred Harp Convention has been cancelled for 2020. Big Creek PBC has agreed to host for next year. The convention will be October 30/31 in 2021.

December

December 5th and 6th, First Sunday and Saturday before - the 11th GA State Christian Harmony Convention is cancelled for this year. We’ll plan to meet on December 4/5 in 2021.

December 5th and 6th, First Sunday and Saturday before - Florida State Convention. Per Stanley Smith they will be having the convention this year. (Cooper Book) It will be at the Sacred Harp Singing Hall near Millers Crossroads / same location as the last two years. Most people stay in Enterprise Alabama at the Fairfield Inn or the Candlewood suites. If anyone needs more details have them text or call Oscar McGuire at 478-955-6135.

The Candlelight Nights program at the Atlanta History Center has been cancelled for this year. Thanks to all those that have helped with this in the past.

January

January 1 - THe New Year's Day Georgian Harmony singing held at the home of John and Elsie Hollingsworth is cancelled for this year. Plan to join us Jan 1, 2022!
