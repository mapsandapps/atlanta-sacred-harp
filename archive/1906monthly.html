June 2019 Singings

Updated June 1, 2019

The Summer singing season is heating up! There are numerous opportunities to sing this month, including some singings from the Christian Harmony.

Please make special note of Holly Springs this Saturday and Sunday, the Alpharetta June Singing on June 9, and the DeLong-Roberts Memorial Singing on June 23 - all within an easy drive from anywhere in Metro Atlanta.

NOTE: () The 2nd Tuesday Monthly Singing at Holy Trinity in Decatur will be suspended for the months of June and July. We will reconvene on the 2nd Tuesday of August, with childcare available! Info: Lisa Bennett, ltb1014 -at- gmail -dot- com.

It's not too late to register for Camp Fasola (Youth Emphasis). Go to campfasola.org for more info.

All singings are from The Sacred Harp 1991 edition unless otherwise noted. For Google maps for all these singings, please see the links on the pages for annual singings, local groups or note singings.
