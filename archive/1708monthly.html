August 2017 Singings

Updated August 1, 2017

Your august presence is required at these wonderful singings. Note: The 3rd Sunday of August, normally held at Cedar Creek, will be held at Mt. Pisgah instead this year. Also note special opportunities at the "Songs Abound" session at David and Andy's house this coming Friday, and the Little 5 Points "Arts Alive" singing on the 12th.

Aug. 3, 1st Thurs., monthly singing, 7:30-9:30, in the Robinson Fellowship Hall, behind Emory Presbyterian Church, 1886 North Decatur Rd, Atlanta GA 30307. Directions: Half-mile west of the intersection of North Decatur Rd and Clairmont Rd, at the corner of North Decatur Rd and Westminster Way. Parking behind the church, off Westminster Way. Experienced and new singers, all are welcome. Contact: David Brodeur, david .at. davidbrodeur .dot. com.

August 4, Friday, Songs Abound #5: We'll Always Have Parris, 7:00-9:00 PM, hosted by Andy Ditzler, for location please email andy-at-andyditzler-dot-com. New compositions in the Sacred Harp style by local and international composers, a historical Sacred Harp lesson, and extended lessons from alternate tune books. This month our guest singing master Jesse P. Karlsberg will lead an extended lesson devoted to the songs of O. A. Parris. We will sing an early fuging version of Nativity (350), and songs by Jesse Karlsberg, John Bayer, and others.

Alternate route: I-20W to exit #30/Post Road (just past Douglasville), go south until you get to Highway 166, turn right onto 166...you'll stay on this for a good ways, don't worry about any turns until you get into Carroll County. It will eventually intersect with Cross Plains Road and when it does, turn left onto Hutcheson Ferry Road (the dirt road). Wilson's Chapel will be on the left. John's been going this way a few years, thinks it might be a little shorter.
