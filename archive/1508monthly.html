August 2015 Singings

Updated July 31, 2015


Don't let the August heat wilt your desire to experience really good singing... it's always better when we get the fans going in time with the music!

Looking ahead - as many of you know we'll be hosting the United Convention again this year at Church of Our Saviour in Virginia-Highlands, September 12th/13th. If you are interested/able/willing to host singers visiting from out of town....please let us know. Send a note to mail -at- atlantasacredharp -dot- org and let us know (how many you could host, etc).

Directions: From Athens: Take Hwy 29 north, towards Danielsville. At the light near the Ingles store, bear left onto Hwy 106. At the next light, about 1 mile, turn right to stay on 106 toward Ila. Just past the 9-mile marker, turn left onto Adams-Clark Road (at the Hill's Automotive sign). Go 1.5 miles to mailbox on right numbered 1574. When driveway splits, bear left. Turn right and go 0.8 mile, then turn right on Adams- Clark Road, go 1.5 miles to mailbox on right, no. 1574; when driveway splits, bear left. Contact: John Hollingsworth, john -at- billnet.org, (706) 789-3916.

Aug. 1 and 2, Sat. and 1st Sun., 163rd Chattahoochee Sacred Harp Convention, Wilson's Chapel, 289 Hutcheson Ferry Rd, Whitesburg, Ga 30185, southeast of Carrollton, Ga. 9:30 a.m. John Plunkett's directions: Take I-20 to Hwy 61, south to Carrollton. Turn left at the 166 by- pass (there are 2 places to turn onto 166, take the 2nd turn...it's a big intersection), go about 1 mile and turn left (south) on Old Newnan Road. Old Newnan will eventually come to a fork and bear to the right, you want to go straight (left) onto Cross Plains Road. Go past Cross Plains Baptist Church and the old School House; at the 3 way intersection jog slightly to the right and take the dirt road, which is Hutcheson Ferry Road, Wilson's Chapel is about 100 yards down the dirt road on the left. Alternate route: I-20W to exit #30/Post Road (just past Douglasville), go south until you get to Highway 166, turn right onto 166...you'll stay on this for a good ways, don't worry about any turns until you get into Carroll County. Look out for Cross Plains-Hulett Road...(there is a convenience store on the right)...turn left onto Cross Plains-Hulett Road. It will eventually intersect with Cross Plains Road and when it does, turn left onto Hutcheson Ferry Road (the dirt road). Wilson's Chapel will be on the left. John's been going this way a few years, thinks it might be a little shorter. GPS: 33.557058, -84.966422 Google maps: 272 Hutcheson Ferry Rd Whitesburg, GA 30185 Info: John Plunkett, bildad12 -at-hotmail.com, 470-230-5757.
