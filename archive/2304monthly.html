April 2023 Singings

Updated April 1, 2023

Some good opportunities to sing this month!

Apr. 1, Saturday before the 1st Sunday, 10:00a-3:00p. NEW All day singing at St. Elizabeth’s Episcopal Church, 1188 Hamp Mill Rd, Dahlonega, GA, All are welcome at the first annual Dahlonega all-day Sacred Harp singing. We will begin singing from The Sacred Harp (1991) at 10 am and conclude around 3 pm. There will be a dinner on the grounds at noon. Participants are invited to bring dishes. A full kitchen is available. This is an indoor singing. We will be taking COVID precautions to protect our vulnerable community members. We ask that participants take an at-home test either before departing or upon arrival (tests will be available on-site beginning at 9 am). Please do not come if you are experiencing symptoms.

Apr. 2, 1st Sunday, 9:30 am Central Time, Edwardsville Baptist Church, Edwardsville, AL. 6 miles west of Fruithurst, just off U.S. Hwy. 78 (about 10 miles west of the Georgia - Alabama line). contact: Cecil Roberts (770) 574-7832

Test at home, and bring the details to be verified. Tests are also available on site, if you arrive early. Apr. 8, Saturday before the 2nd Sunday, 10:00am (ET). The State Line singing will be held on the Saturday before the 2nd Sunday in April, April 8, at 9:00 CT, 10:00 ET. However, for this year only, circumstances dictate that we hold the singing at Holly Springs Church in Bremen, GA, instead of at State Line. Due to lack of use during the Covid years, there are repairs that need to be made to State Line Church in order to make it usable for an April singing in Alabama. We really appreciate the generosity of Judy Henry and her family for allowing us to use Holly Springs on such short notice. We do, however, plan on having the singing at State Line next year. We hope to see you there. Please bring a covered dish for lunch.
