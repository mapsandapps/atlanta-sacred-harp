December 2017 Singings

Updated December 2, 2017

Notes: There will be no "first Thursday" singing at Emory Presbyterian this month. Join us for the annual Atlanta Sacred Harp Holiday gathering on the 17th. Details below.

There will NOT be a New Years Eve singing at the Hollingsworths' this year.

We’ve been invited to participate in the “Candlelight Nights” event at the Atlanta History Center, for Friday, Dec 15th and Friday, Dec 22nd. We will sing a 30 minute set at 7p, 8p, 9p. If you are able to help with this, please let John Plunkett know at bildad12-at-hotmail-dot-com.

Upcoming, mark your calendars: there will be a singing school/all day singing on Saturday, January 13th at Lake Oconee Lutheran Church (1089 Greensboro Rd, Eatonton, GA 31024). 10 a.m. -3 p.m. Covered dish lunch at noon. Nathan Rees will teach the singing school. It would be great to have some experienced singers to support Nathan in the singing school, as well as when we move into more of a regular singing format.
