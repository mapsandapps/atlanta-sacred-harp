Mar. 14, Mon., Songs Abound, 7:00 pm at the home of David Brodeur and Andy Ditzler in Atlanta. Please contact andy -at- andyditzler dot com for address and directions. Songs Abound is an occasional series of singings at the home of David Brodeur and Andy Ditzler in Atlanta. At each Songs Abound event we sing new compositions in the Sacred Harp style by local and international composers, and a guest leader will teach an extended lesson from an alternate tune book. Songs Abound events are an enjoyable way to improve our sight-reading skills, allow composers to hear their tunes, and deepen our knowledge of Sacred Harp singing. This is the first Songs Abound session. In the first half we will sing new compositions. In the second half, guest leader Lauren Bock will teach an extended lesson from the Northern Harmony.
