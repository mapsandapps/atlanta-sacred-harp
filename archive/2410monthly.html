Sacred Harp Family - many of you will remember Bob Goodman, who was a very active singer until 2016 or so. His long-time companion, Janet Wuichet, called me today to pass along the sad news of Bob's death, on Sept 27, in California, where his children had moved him. She has asked if some singers could come to Bob's memorial potluck on the evening of Sunday, Oct 13, 6-10 pm, at the First Existentialist Church. There are more details in the email below. I spoke with one of the organizers, Stephanie Coffin, today and she asked if one of us could give a brief explanation of how shape note singing works, and for us to sing a few songs. There will be other singers there from various groups Bob belonged to, and many of them might wish to join in. I thought we could print up a small packet of songs to share. We do have a limited amount of time, so we have to make any explanation very brief, and we will only have time for about 4 songs, at a guess. (Another group will be leading the attendees in Amazing Grace.)

I'll serve as point person on this for a rough head count.
Please let me know if you plan to attend.

Warmest regards - Lisa

Original email with a few more details:

BOB GOODMAN MEMORIAL POTLUCK

Friends—

Longtime activist Bob Goodman died on September 27 in California, far from his Atlanta home. Knowing that Bob no longer has family here, a group of old friends decided that a potluck get together in his honor and memory might be appropriate. We have reserved space on

Sunday, October 13, 2024
6 — 10 pm
First Existentialist Church
470 Candler Park Dr
Atlanta, GA

We are trying to contact as many people as possible who knew Bob. We are contacting Great Speckled Bird folk, October League cadre, Georgia Peace and Justice Coalition activists, Atlanta Grandmothers for Peace, the First Existentialist Church congregation, Shape Note and other singing groups, and various individuals. If you know of people or organizations that knew and worked with Bob, please help out by contacting them. Don't worry about duplication.

A program has not yet been worked out beyond an opening potluck dinner. Please keep dishes on the simpler side (as in “finger food”) to ease clean-up.

After dinner we anticipate some group singing and sing-along, followed by personal reflections on and/or stories about Bob. That should top off the evening, we think.

An advance head count might be useful. If you anticipate attending, please drop a text to (404) 947-5366 or an email to tomcoffin@mac.com.

Please mark the date and plan to come!!
