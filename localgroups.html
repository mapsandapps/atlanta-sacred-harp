Local Singings in Georgia

Singings have been in flux since the pandemic began, and we cannot guarantee the accuracy of information on this page. See our Upcoming Singings page for verified current information.

Athens · Atlanta · Dahlonega · Hoboken · Savannah · Valdosta

There are several local shape-note singing groups in Georgia that meet four or more times a year for about 2 hours at a time. These singings are less formal than all-day singings. Since they meet frequently and tend to attract the same group of people each time, they offer an especially comfortable environment for practicing singing and leading skills. Newcomers are always welcome.

Be aware that locations can change and singings can be rescheduled or cancelled. Check our upcoming singings page for the latest information.

For information or directions, please contact Shannon Primm at sprimm -at- yahoo.com or see the "Athens, Georgia Sacred Harp Sing" Facebook group https://www.facebook.com/groups/22285031545/.

Atlanta First Thursday Singing

Our informal First Thursday singing is a good place for beginners in the Atlanta area to learn this style of music. We meet the first Thursday of each month from 7:00 to 9:00 pm in the Emory Presbyterian Church fellowship building, 1886 North Decatur Road, Atlanta, GA 30307. We sing from The Sacred Harp and the Eclectic Harmony. Loaner books available.

Directions: The church is 1/2 mile west of the intersection of Clairmont and North Decatur Road, on your right coming from Clairmont.

  • From I-85: take exit 91, Clairmont Road, and turn south, toward Emory University. Go 3.9 miles to North Decatur Road, turn right onto North Decatur, and go 1/2 mile. The church is at the corner of Westminster Way and North Decatur. Turn right on Westminster and left into the parking lot behind the church. We sing in the education building to the right of the church.
  • From the east side of I-285: take US-78 west (exit 39), which ends by merging with Lawrenceville Highway. Lawrenceville Highway bears right and turns into Scott Boulevard. The next intersection is star-shaped. Turn right onto the major cross street, which is North Decatur. Go 1.4 miles, turn right on Westminster Way, and left into the church parking lot.

This singing sometimes changes date: be sure to check our upcoming singings page. For more information: Jim Neal, Jim dot Neal -at- mail dot com, or mail@atlantasacredharp.org

Dahlonega Singing

This singing usually meets two Tuesdays a month from 7 to 8:45. (Dates adjust to fit the school schedule.) Location is the Vickery House on the campus of the University of North Georgia; park anywhere. Google Maps: https://maps.app.goo.gl/nTDMigDVHK3QBfSd6. For information, contact Esther Morgan-Ellis at emellis -at- gmail -dot- com or request to join the Sacred Harp Dahlonega Facebook group https://www.facebook.com/groups/2813102368995680. In addition to posting on Facebook, Esther keeps an email list and reminds everyone the day before each meeting, and is always happy to add people to the list.

Savannah Monthly Singing

We meet on the Saturday before the Second Sunday each month at the Whitefield Center, 106 East 37th Street, Savannah, Georgia, from 1:00 to 3:00 p.m. Singing is from the 1991 Sacred Harp and the B. F. White Sacred Harp (Cooper book), loaners provided. Double check dates on our Facebook page: https://www.facebook.com/groups/savannahsacredharp or for more information contact Dawn Stanford, (912)484-5868, dawnipawni@gmail.com.
