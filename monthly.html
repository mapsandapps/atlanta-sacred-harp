May 9th - Saturday before the 2nd Sunday - The fifth Pumpkintown Shape Note Singing will be held at the Oolenoy Community House - 5301 Dacusville Hwy (SC 135) Pickens, SC on May 9, 2026, from 10am until 3pm. We will enjoy dinner on the grounds from noon until 1. We will sing from the 2025 edition of the Sacred Harp and the 2010 Edition of the Christian Harmony. Loaners will be available, and we have a few copies of the new Sacred Harp that may be purchased. We sing in a 100-year-old, two-room school house with great acoustics and (hopefully) a refreshing breeze from the nearby mountains. The building is not truly ADA compliant, however there is only one short step up to get into the building, and the small restrooms should be able to accommodate wheelchairs. It is about 13 miles north of Easley, SC on SC Hwy 135, although those traveling from the north or west may want to access it from Scenic Hwy 11. (Check Google Maps for detailed directions.) For more information, please contact Dave Farmer at davefarmer-at-charter-dot-net. You may also call me at (864) 676-9003 (home) or (864) 605-8236 (cell). Please join us May 10th in metropolitan Pumpkintown!
