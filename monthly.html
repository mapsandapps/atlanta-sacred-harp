Atlanta Sacred Harp July 2025 Newsletter

No Emory Presbyterian singing on the 1st Thursday of the month. The “Christmas in July” will happen in conjunction with the Pine Lake Singing on the 3rd Wednesday (16th)

July 5 & 6, 1st Sunday and Saturday before, 9:30 am Central Time both days. Henagar-Union Convention, Liberty Church, 2 miles north of Henagar AL on Liberty Road. From Henagar Crossing, take Hwy 40 west to the Farmers Telephone Cooperative. Turn right and go to the dead end. Turn right, take first left past Henagar Methodist Church (Liberty Rd). Church is on the right, approximately 1.5 miles. Info: david -at- davidivey -dot- com. Social at the Ivey farm following the singing. Bring your instruments!

July 12, Saturday, 30th annual John C Campbell Folk School Singing, 1 Folk School Rd, Brasstown, NC 28902 Location: The Open House (open air pavilion by the Folk School Gardens) Campus Map: folkschool.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/05/campus-map.pdf Singing: 10:00 AM. - 3:00 PM, Dinner on the grounds at Noon. Come sing, learn the shapes, contemplate the poetry, and relish the power of voices raised together in song. These events welcome first-timer and experienced shape note singers to sing from the tune books Christian Harmony and The Sacred Harp. Experience the beauty of singing 4 parts unaccompanied. All shape note singings are free and open to the public. Contact: Bill Hogan - hoganbill56 at gmail dot com

July 16, Wednesday - Pine Lake Sacred Harp Singing “Christmas in July” - We will be singing Christmas songs from the Sacred Harp, An American Christmas Harp, and The Eclectic Harmony. Feel free to bring copies of any 4-shape Christmas songs you like. Holiday snacks and attire encouraged! Singing in the Pine Lake Beach House. Pine Lake Sacred Harp singing, third Wednesday of the month, June 18th, 2025, 7-9 pm Pine Lake Beach House, 4580 Lakeshore Drive,Stone Mountain, GA 30083, Google Maps link: https://maps.app.goo.gl/rZYFqrvA4iAKemTKA, You can also search under "Pine Lake Beach House" on Google Maps, or "Pine Lake Beach" - the latter will take you to the lakeside beach area directly next to the building.

July 17, Thursday - You are warmly invited to attend the 2025 Capital City Singing to be held at Old Alabama Town (Loeb Reception Center) in Montgomery, AL on THURSDAY, July 17, 9:30am - 3pm. We'll sing from four books: The Sacred Harp (1991 Edition), The Sacred Harp (Cooper Revision), The Christian Harmony, and The Colored Sacred Harp. Lunch will be Dutch treat; please plan to bring a sack lunch or enjoy one of Montgomery's tasty restaurants. Please see the full address below: Old Alabama Town, Loeb Reception Center, 301 Columbus Street, Montgomery, AL 36104. Contact - Stefani Priskos - stefani at arts dot alabama dot gov

Sacred Harp 2025 Edition Premiere at the United Convention

The Sacred Harp Publishing Company will release the revised 2025 edition of the Sacred Harp songbook at the United Convention right here in Atlanta, the weekend of September 13-14, 2025. This will be a once-in-a-generation event! There will be hundreds of Sacred Harp singers in Atlanta for this event from across Europe and North America. The singing will be exciting!

The Saturday session will be dedicated to the introduction of the new book and its new songs, recognition of composers, and recognition of the committee and adjunct committees and working groups. The Sunday session will be a regular singing day from the new songbook. Other events will occur throughout the weekend. See the Sacred Harp Publishing Company website for details.
