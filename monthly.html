November 2025 Singings

Updated October 29, 2025

November 2025 Atlanta Sacred Harp singing update:

Nov 1st, Saturday - 10:00am, Denney Memorial at Emmaus Primitive Baptist Church on Oak Grove Church Road, Carrollton, GA. Dinner on the grounds at noon.

Nov 1st, Saturday - Christian Harmony with the Cherokee Language Repertory Choir. 10:00 AM-3:00 PM, Saturday, November 1, 2025. COUNCIL HOUSE, New Echota State Historic Site, 1211 Hwy 225 NE, Calhoun, GA 30701. This stretch of Highway 225 may show up as Chatsworth Road on some map services. To find the Council House from the parking area, put the museum on your left-hand side, walk past the homestead, then take the leftward path; Council House will be on your right and up a few stairs. Book: The Christian Harmony, 2010 edition. Boy Scouts are providing hamburgers, hot dogs, soda-pop, etc. This singing is part of the Cherokee Heritage Day festival at New Echota, onetime capitol of the Cherokee Nation in northwest Georgia. Admission to park with a Georgia DNR Historic Site Pass or a la carte ($8 general, $6 youth/seniors, small children free). Sara Snyder Hopkins will bring everything you need to sing Christian Harmony in Cherokee. There will likely be multiple "explanations" throughout the day as festival-goers wander in and out. And singers are, of course, welcome to duck out to enjoy the games, living history demonstrations, building tours, etc. CONTACTS: singing: Sara Snyder Hopkins slsnyder at email dot wcu dot edu; site/festival: Judy Mincey mincey dot judy at yahoo dot com

Nov 2nd, Sunday - 10:00am, Holly Springs Memorial Singing at Holly Springs Primitive Baptist Church, Bremen, GA. Dinner on the grounds at noon.

Nov 4th & 19th - Tuesday or Wednesday, 7:00pm - Dahlonega Sacred Harp singing, We sing at the Vickery House on West Main Street, Dahlonega, GA. The Vickery House does not have a street address, but it is on Google Maps. Since we sing in the evenings, you can park in any UNG spot or right next to the back door of the Vickery House. We start with a brief introduction for newcomers at 6:45. Then we sing until at least 8:45, although sometimes we go a bit longer. All are welcome! Contact Esther Morgan-Ellis - emellis at gmail dot com. Last singing of this semester will be Dec 2nd.

Nov 6th, First Thursday - 7:00 PM to 9:00 PM. singing from The 2025 Sacred Harp and The Eclectic Harmony. Emory Presbyterian Church, 1886 North Decatur Rd, Atlanta GA 30307. Park behind the church, off Westminster Way. Info:Jim Neal, jim_neal-at-bellsouth.net.

Nov 8th, Saturday before the Second Sunday, The ~29th Swannanoa Valley Singing. 10 AM-3 PM, Saturday. ST. JAMES EPISCOPAL CHURCH, Black Mountain, NC. Carolina book: morning; CH2010: afternoon. Books: The Christian Harmony ('Carolina book', 1873 facsimile, 2015 Folk Heritage reprint); The Christian Harmony, 2010 edition. CONTACT: Joe Holbert - treeblow at bellsouth dot net. https://www.christianharmony.org/schedule.html

Nov 15th, Saturday - 10:00am start. All day Georgian Harmony Singing at No. 3 Railroad Street, Arnoldsville, GA. Dinner on the grounds at noon. 10:00 a.m.-3:00 p.m.Contact/info: Oscar McGuire . 478-955-6135 (text preferred) or Michael Spencer - fishman.mds at gmail dot com. http://www.3railroad.org/index.php

Nov 17th, 3rd Monday Atlanta Singing, 7:30pm. In The Barn behind St. John the Wonderworker 543 Cherokee Ave SE, Atlanta, GA 30312. The Barn- off of Orleans Avenue, walk up drive way behind the church. No musical experience needed. Contact info: Anna Hinton annajhinton at icloud dot com.

Nov 22nd & 23rd, Alabama Sacred Harp Musical Convention, Southside Community Center, 2142 Highway 77, Southside, Alabama 35907. Southside is located in the Gadsden area, south of Rainbow City on Alabama Highway 77. Dinner on the grounds. You are welcome to bring food for lunch. Sacred Harp Musical Heritage Association (SHMHA) Annual Meeting follows Saturday singing session. Info: David Ivey, David at DavidIvey dot com or 256-258-8660

WE ARE BLESSED TO ANNOUNCE The 200th Anniversary Service at Hardeman Primitive Baptist Church 3965 Glenwood Rd. Decatur GA. 30032. We cordially invite all to join with us on Saturday 8th, November and Sunday the 9th. to celebrate the 200th anniversary of the constitution of the church. Services will begin at 10:00 in the morning, with congregational singing at followed by preaching of the gospel, fellowship and prayer in service to our Lord. Following the morning service we will have lunch in the Fellowship Hall. Song and Preaching service will continue after lunch. As we continue to enjoy God's blessings at Hardeman Church we invite you to come share in those blessings and help us celebrate this special occasion.
