Hi, Friends! Just a reminder that we are hosting the United Convention and 2025 Sacred Harp Revision Debut singing on September 13-14 (not to mention a day-long symposium on the revision process on Friday September 12 at Emory). Some folks would really love to come but housing in Atlanta, as we all know, is not cheap.

Hosting travelers is a time-honored tradition in Sacred Harp and can be really fun. I know many of you probably already have full houses for the United, but if you have some space to share for at least Saturday and Sunday, please fill out the "housing to offer" form and Lauren Bock and I will try to match up singers with appropriate hosts. Lots of the folks who really need housing are young and would be thrilled with a couch or even a space on the floor to roll out a sleeping bag.

So far, we have only had two folks fill out the form. If you have been meaning to, now would be a great time to do so. It's not that long and your space doesn't have to be fancy.

Thanks for helping to maintain our reputation for hospitality!

Lisa

Sacred Harp 2025 Edition Premiere at the United Convention

The Sacred Harp Publishing Company will release the revised 2025 edition of the Sacred Harp songbook at the United Convention right here in Atlanta, the weekend of September 13-14, 2025. This will be a once-in-a-generation event! There will be hundreds of Sacred Harp singers in Atlanta for this event from across Europe and North America. The singing will be exciting!

The Saturday session will be dedicated to the introduction of the new book and its new songs, recognition of composers, and recognition of the committee and adjunct committees and working groups. The Sunday session will be a regular singing day from the new songbook. Other events will occur throughout the weekend. See the Sacred Harp Publishing Company website for details.
